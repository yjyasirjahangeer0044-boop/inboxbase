## Where the free plan limits hit

Honesty matters in a free plan review, so let's cover what you don't get:

- **Brevo logo in your emails.** Free senders show a "Sent with Brevo" footer. To remove it, you upgrade to any paid plan or buy the $9/month logo removal add-on.
- **A/B testing.** This unlocks on Standard ($18/mo). For most beginners, you don't need A/B testing in month one anyway.
- **Landing pages.** Standard plan onwards.
- **Send-time optimization** (AI feature). Standard plan onwards.
- **Phone support.** Free users get email support only. Response times are reasonable but not instant.
- **Multi-user access.** One user only on free. Standard adds up to 3 users.
- **Advanced segmentation.** Free includes basic segmentation; advanced behavior-based segments need Standard or Professional.
- **300 emails per day cap.** If you have a 5,000-contact list and want to send a single newsletter to everyone, you can't. You'd need to upgrade to Starter ($9) to remove the daily cap.

For the vast majority of people just starting email marketing, none of these limits matter for the first 60–90 days.

To be fair, the free plan isn't perfect for everyone. Skip it if:

**You send daily newsletters to more than 300 subscribers.** The 300/day cap will hit you on day one. You'll need Starter ($9/month) immediately, which is still cheap but no longer "free."

**You need to remove the Brevo logo from email footers right away.** The free plan shows "Sent with Brevo" in the footer. For most personal newsletters this is fine. For professional client work, it looks unprofessional. Starter or higher removes it.

**You need phone support.** Free and Starter plans get email support only. If you're running a business where every campaign matters and you need someone on the phone to troubleshoot, Professional ($499) is the entry point for phone support.

**You're running a high-volume transactional system.** The free plan's daily cap applies to all sends including transactional. If your app sends thousands of password reset and order confirmation emails daily, jump straight to a paid plan.
